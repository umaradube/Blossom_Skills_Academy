Blossom Skills Academy was founded with a single conviction: quality healthcare training should be accessible to every South African who wants to serve their community. Based in Durban, we design our programmes to fit around your responsibilities — whether you are a working parent, a career-changer, or a school leaver taking your first step.

Our blended learning model means you study theory on your own schedule, then build real competency through supervised practical sessions right here in Durban.

Hands-On Training

Where Theory Meets
Practice — In Durban

Our in-person practical sessions are the heart of the Caregiver Course. While you complete your theory entirely online, these supervised sessions ensure you are truly job-ready before you graduate.

Scheduled Sessions

Practical dates are announced in advance so you can plan your attendance. Sessions are held on weekends and select weekdays.

Qualified Supervision

All practical sessions are led by registered healthcare professionals with real-world caregiving experience.

Competency-Based Assessment

You are assessed on practical skills — patient handling, personal care, emergency response — to ensure you meet certification standards.

Durban Venue

Conveniently located in Durban, KwaZulu-Natal. Exact venue details are shared upon enrollment.

Attendance is Mandatory

All students must attend the scheduled practical sessions in Durban to be eligible for certification. Failure to attend may delay your graduation date.
